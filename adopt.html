Our Adoptable Cats:



Our adoptable cats come from our partner rescue, Long Beach Felines. LBF ensures that all of our kitties are spayed/neutered, microchipped, dewormed, flea-free, vaccinated, and negative for FIV and feline leukemia. Best of all, all of our kitties are very social with people and one another! Fill out an application today!
